Welcome to my humble animal shelter, here you will find many different cute, smart animals but abandoned by their owners, and sooner or later they will get new owners, and you can help with this!

Garry cat

Garry is a very affectionate and good cat, he will misbehave but only out of love for you. He likes to eat well and sleep a lot, likes to play with a laser and a toy mouse. We are sure that you will be happy with a cat like him!

Charlie the dog

Charlie is a kind and brave dog. He is also blind, but this does not make him unhappy, we are sure that new good owners will make him happy, because even though he is blind, he loves to play with the ball!

Doolitle hamster

Doolitleis a small, funny and mischievous hamster that will make your life unforgettable! Although he is old and still runs vigorously, steals seeds and loves to chew his fingers, but this is all from a great love for people!
